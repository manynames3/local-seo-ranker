Privacy

Data handling for rank scans and workspaces.

Local SEO Ranker stores account email, workspace details, scan inputs, generated reports, credit usage, and session records needed to operate the service.

Live lookup provider credentials stay on the server. Browser code never receives the provider API key. Live scan inputs may be sent to the configured Maps/SERP provider to return ranking and location results.

Reports and history are scoped to the signed-in workspace. Exported files are created in your browser when you choose Copy, JSON, CSV, or Print.

Do not enter regulated personal data, health data, payment card data, or private customer records into scan notes. Use business and market information only.
